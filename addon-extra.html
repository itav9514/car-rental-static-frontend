Premium Emergency Roadside Service

Reduce your financial liability for non-mechanical car issues and unexpected roadside service costs such as missing key, lockout, dead battery, spare tires, empty fuel tank, and more when you add Premium Emergency Roadside Service (PERS).

Emergency Roadside Service (PERS) benefits:

  • Lock-out service – maximum $250 per rental
  • Remote unlock – maximum $105 per rental
  • Damaged/broken key, fob, key card – maximum $500 per rental
  • Lost/missing key, fob, key card – maximum $500 per rental
  • Keys, fob, key card (Delivery/Towing) – maximum $300 per rental
  • Spare tire mounting service – maximum $250 per rental
  • No spare tire (Towing) – maximum $500 per rental
  • Dead battery (Left light/accessory on) – maximum $250 per rental
  • Out of EV range/charge service (Towing to a charging station)* – maximum $350 per rental
  • Out of EV range/charge service (EV range delivery) – maximum $250 per rental
  • Out of gas fuel service – maximum $250 per rental
  • Car stuck/winch service – maximum $500 per rental
  • Missing/stolen/damaged EV charging cable/adapter

If costs exceed the maximum amounts described above, the renter will be responsible for such excess. The cost, coverage, provider, availability and terms of Emergency Roadside Service may vary by location. Please inquire at the counter for additional information. Requirements for corporate account customers may vary. Please contact the appropriate department.

Protect yourself from liability

Protect yourself from liability

  • Liability Insurance Supplement (LIS) covers third-party automobile liability claims for bodily injury and property damage for rentals in the U.S.
  • If purchased, LIS will provide coverage before your personal policy and covers your liability to third parties, resulting from an auto accident in the rented vehicle, for bodily injury and property damage (other than to the rental vehicle).
  • Maximum LIS protection up to $300,000* for each accident for bodily injury and property damage in excess of minimum underlying state statutory limits and, up to $100,000 for uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage for bodily injury per accident in excess of minimum underlying state statutory limits. For rentals commencing in California, no uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage will be provided with the purchase of LIS. There is no coverage for an uninsured/underinsured motorist claim as a result of negligence by the rental driver.
  • *LIS limits may vary in certain states, and terms and conditions apply. Ask your rental representative. Pricing of LIS for a specific rental will be provided at time of reservation.

Bypass toll booths with ease.

Toll roads? Travel assured. Save money, bypass toll booths and drive seamlessly through electronic toll lanes when you add our all-inclusive PlatePass® option.*

All-inclusive PlatePass tolling benefits:

  • Unlimited, 24-7 toll usage throughout the extent of your rental for a low daily rate (rate varies by state).
  • No waiting in long lines searching for coins, cash or waiting for receipts.
  • No paying tolls during or after your rental.
  • Ability to use most electronic tollways and bridges, without using a personal transponder or paying the toll authority directly.
  • Convenient as many toll roads no longer provide traffic booths to pay tolls in person.
  • PlatePass all-inclusive tolling is available in California, Colorado, Delaware, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Maine, Massachusetts, Maryland, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, Tennessee, Texas, Vermont, Virginia, Washington State and West Virginia.

Customers can opt into the optional PlatePass service in 3 ways:

  • At the commencement of the rental with an all-inclusive rate per day or per week that covers the cost of tolls
  • At return
  • By opening the transponder shield box (E-Z Pass pass transponder region only) when applicable.

If you decline optional all-inclusive tolling PlatePass service at the start of your rental period but still use electronic toll roads and/or bridges during the rental period (including “cashless” or “all electronic” toll roads and bridges) you will be liable for and we will charge you for: (a) all tolls incurred for such use at the highest, undiscounted applicable toll rate; (b) a $9.99 usage day fee and (c) all other applicable toll charges or fees, if any.
